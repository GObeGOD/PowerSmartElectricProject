At Power Smart Electric, we work hard to bring innovation and qualified experience together to build community. Power Smart Electric is committed to building a more sustainable future through modeling environmental awareness and responsibility. We take our environmental commitment very seriously, and have outlined a number of policies and best practices in our everyday actions to minimize the impact of our activities on the environment.

Request a quote.

Power Smart Electric Ltd. is committed to:

  • Actively promoting recycling both internally and amongst our customers and suppliers.
  • Meeting or exceeding all environmental legislation that relates to the Company.
  • Minimizing waste by evaluating operations and ensuring they are as efficient as possible.
  • Sourcing and promoting product ranges to minimise the environmental impact of both production and distribution.
  • Moving towards completing HVAC upgrades at our head office, demonstrating reduced cooling and heating loads.
  • Utilizing electric lifts and battery-charged forklifts where practicable.
  • Designing custom maintenance packages for businesses to maximize product life and efficiency.
