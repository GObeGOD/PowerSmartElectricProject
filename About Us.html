About Us

Power Smart Electric

Power Smart Electric Ltd. Power Smart Electric is an Ottawa-owned, class-A unrestricted electrical contractor operating since 2000. We are a family-owned and operated company and have experience in residential, commercial, hi-rise, retrofit, LED lighting design and installation, and ecological retrofit projects. As an electrical contractor operating in the Ottawa Valley and beyond, we count many of the new downtown Ottawa hi-rises as part of our past projects and are proud to play a part in this world-class city’s changing skyline. Power Smart Electric is a member of the Power Smart Alliance, the Ontario Electrical Association, and the Better Business Bureau of Ottawa.
